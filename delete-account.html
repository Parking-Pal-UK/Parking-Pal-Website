Delete Account

Parking Pal users can delete their account directly inside the app.

Delete your account in the app

To delete your account, sign in to Parking Pal and go to:

Account → Delete Account & Remove My Data

You will be asked to confirm before your account is deleted.

What happens when your account is deleted?

Your personal account information, including your profile and login details, will be removed from our systems.

Comments, parking submissions, location updates and history records will remain in anonymised form so the community parking database remains useful. Comments can be deleted manually before deleting your account if required.

If you cannot access your account

If you are unable to sign in to the app or you want specific data removed without deleting your enitire account, you can request manual account deletion by emailing:

support@parkingpal.uk

Please contact us from the email address linked to your Parking Pal account where possible.
