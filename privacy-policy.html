Privacy Policy

Parking Pal is a community-driven truck parking database designed to help drivers find suitable parking locations across the United Kingdom and Ireland.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name and any information you choose to add to your profile.

We also collect information you submit to the app including comments, parking location submissions, location updates, food options and feedback.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide and improve the Parking Pal service, moderate community content and maintain the parking database.

Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the app by navigating to the Account section and selecting Delete Account.

Personal account information is removed from our systems. Community contributions such as comments, location submissions and update history may remain in anonymised form to preserve the integrity of the database.
